About Soil Analyst App

The Soil Analyst App aims to provide a user-friendly tool for testing and analyzing different properties of soils in the African continent. Inspired by the need for accurate soil analysis in agriculture, and empowering average farmers and researchers who lack the financial capacity to carry out expensive and ellaborate laboratory analysis, I developed this app, which will aid them in making informed decisions.

About Soil Analyst App

The Soil Analyst App is a software engineering student portfolio project that aims to provide a user-friendly tool for testing and analyzing different properties of soils in the African continent. Inspired by the need for accurate soil analysis in agriculture, our team developed this app to assist farmers and researchers in making informed decisions.

This project was developed as part of the Portfolio Project for ALX Africa. To learn more about the team members and their contributions, visit our LinkedIn, GitHub, and Twitter profiles:
